Please help bP celebrate her 47th birthday by walking, jogging, biking or some other form of movement. Commit to a 5k or 47 minutes of your favorite activity between March 7th and March 13th! If you are able donate to the fund.

Share your movement on social media using the hashtag #GiveMoreWithA74!

Bethanie Perry is an Assistant Professor of history at Northern Virginia Community College and is passionate not simply about teaching history but making sure her students can have access to the tools and resources that help them overcome their daily challenges outside of the classroom. This movement event began as a brainstorming between sisters on how bP could continue to demonstrate her compassion and energy towards her community.

The #oneNOVA Student Emergency Fund provides emergency grants of $500 to support base needs insecurities like food, shelter, medical expenses and technology needed for remote learning.
